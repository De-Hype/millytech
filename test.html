Our Programmes

Our programmes are designed to cater to different age groups, both youths and kids

Tech-Up a Girl (TAG)

Empowering young girls with tech skills, boosting their confidence and self-esteem, and enhancing their chances of securing remote jobs both locally and internationally.

Kids Can Code (KCC)

Empowering young kids with tech skills to develop an interest for tech so as they grow, they can choose to pursue a career in tech.
