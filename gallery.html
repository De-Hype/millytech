Frequently Asked Questions

We try to answer most common questions, if you have any additional, please get in touch with our friendly team

01. What is Milly-Tech Vision all about?

Our Mission is to empower girls, youths, and children through tech education.

02. How experienced are your tutors?

Our tutors each have over 5 years of experience and have worked with local and international clients, and are therefore in the best position to pass on their knowledge to students looking to go into tech.

03. What is your teaching method?

At MillyTech, we provide a classroom for group learning and also offer virtual classes.

04. How can I contact Milly-Tech Vision?

How can I contact MillyTech?** You can reach us via email at hello@milly-tech.vision, call us at +234 806 830 8405, or visit us at Enen Afaha Street, Uyo, Akwa Ibom State.

05. Does Milly-Tech Vision truly offer free tech courses?

Yes, Milly-Tech Vision genuinely offers free tech courses, giving everyone the opportunity to gain valuable skills without the financial burden. It's our way of empowering the next generation of tech innovators.
